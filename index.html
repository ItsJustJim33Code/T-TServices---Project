What we do

At T&T Vehicle Center, we offer a wide range of services to cater to all your automotive needs. Our offerings include MOT testing to ensure your vehicle meets safety and environmental standards, regular servicing to keep your vehicle running smoothly, and mechanical repairs for any faults, whether minor or major. We also provide tyre services, including changing, balancing, and aligning tyres for optimal performance, as well as recovery services for roadside assistance and vehicle recovery.

Who are we

At T&T Vehicle Services, we are passionate car enthusiasts dedicated to getting your vehicle back on the road quickly and in top condition. Our experienced mechanics use the latest diagnostic tools to ensure efficient and high-quality repairs. We treat each car with the utmost care, aiming to exceed your expectations with our exceptional service. Trust us to keep your car running smoothly and safely, so you can focus on the road ahead.

About T&T Vehicle center

We have been in business now for 6 years and run a medium sized garage at the top of Hayling Island. We have been expanding overtime and now have mutiple facilities to accomodate multiple vehicles at any one time, along with being able to facilitate many different type of vehicles from small to large and many different needs from our customers.
